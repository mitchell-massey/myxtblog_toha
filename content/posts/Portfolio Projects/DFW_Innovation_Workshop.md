> At 900lbs, we’re grateful to collaborate with some of the world’s most innovative teams, and recently we had the privilege of partnering with the DFW Airport Innovation team to facilitate an Innovation Workshop! Together, we explored how immersive technologies can tackle challenges and elevate the passenger and employee experience. The creativity and potential solutions that emerged were truly inspiring. Workshop participants were able to get a look at immersive tech solutions 900lbs has developed for other clients, sparking even more fresh ideas. We'd like to extend a huge thanks to Maria Brackenridge and the DFW team for an inspiring, collaborative day.

## About the Client

The DFW Airport innovation team, drives tech-enabled improvements to enhance passenger experience and operational efficiency. Key focus areas include AI, biometrics, 5G, and autonomous systems, often utilizing the "Everyday Innovator" program to foster internal staff innovation.

| Key Focus Areas and Projects | Descriptions|
| -------- | -------- |
|**Technology & AI**| Implementing AI for predictive maintenance, passenger queue management, and advanced security.
   |**Autonomous Technology**| Preparing for autonomous vehicles, including Waymo in terminal areas, and developing AI-enabled wayfinding.
|**Digital Transformation**|Utilizing a digital twin platform combined with IoT sensors for 3D mapping and emergency management.
| **Construction Innovation**|Using modular construction for terminals A, C, and F to reduce operational impact.
|**Future Travel**|Exploring electric vertical takeoff and landing (eVTOL) aircraft.|

## The Challenge

In Summer of 2025, DFW Airport invited 900lbs to direct and facilitate an innovation workshop for 5x airport business units (incl. HR, Employee Experience & Training, Training, Customer Experience, and Airline / Retail).

1 employee from 900lbs and 1 from DFW Innovation Team would be at each of 5 stations, 1 per business unit.

The required Fall 2025 workshop lasted 3 hours with the goal of developing outcomes from insights of the participants - regarding "How might DFW Airport business units leverage immersive technologies to improve airport experience and deliver business outcomes?"

## The Solution

Over several weeks, 900lbs worked intimately with DFW Airport Innovation team to develop a workshop.

0. Generate Proposal and Resource Plan
1. Design Thinking / Innovation Workshop Research / Options Design
2. Audience Assessment (studying the business units)
3. Preparing Workshop Agenda and Physical Materials
4. Preparing demos and presentation content
5. Merging in client slides.
6. Onsite Setup / Tear-down
7. Workshop facilitation
8. Post Event Findings Summary Report & Recommendations

## My Role

- As the lead strategist, I lead the design thinking workshop design, audience assessment, preparing the agenda and slides, and ensuring each workshop station group had materials and workflows to have a great session.
- Post-Event, I drafted the summary findings from each group.

## The Results

- The feedback was immense. Each business unit gave strong satisfaction scores.
- Over 200 ideas were generated, and 4-10 were developed into Innovation Team opportunity areas for solutions in 2026.
- Our rapid planning and reporting ensured a productive and highly responsive partnership.
